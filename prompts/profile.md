# Writer Style Profile Prompt

You are helping prepare reusable writing guidance for Blog Writer.

Read the Markdown files in `samples/` and produce:

1. `writer-style/writer-profile.md`
   - Summarize the author's tone, structure, title habits, pacing, and recurring expressions.
2. `writer-style/style-rules.md`
   - Convert the profile into concise rules future generation tasks must follow.

Preserve the author's natural style. Do not invent a new brand voice.
